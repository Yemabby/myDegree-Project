Program

Maranatha Institutes programs are designed for a new generation of storytellers and computer proteges: artists who share a passion for visual and performing arts across a wide array of media and want to learn by making their own projects in a hands-on, intensive program. Also, for those who have unquenched thirst for computer knowledge. Our students come from across the country and all over the world to learn and work together in Fine Arts, computer courses, professional courses, Degree programs, Conservatory One and Two-Year programs and short-term workshops*. In the creation of our curricula, we worked with faculty and professionals from around the world to develop the most effective curricula available to students. Programs Offered

Maranatha Institutes is more than a computer institute or film school, rather, it is a conservatory of computer, visual and performing arts offering numerous programs that are designed to educate students in a wide variety of disciplines. It has become a creative proving ground for students around the world interested in learning the computer programs, craft of photography, video game design, musical theatre and 3D animation. We attract inspiring, experienced, professional faculty from IT world, media and entertainment industry to guide our students through learning the foundation knowledge and skills of these disciplines. At Maranatha Institute, students can enroll in the following programs to receive intensive, hands-on education that will provide them with practical skills and know-how while also advancing their understanding of computer languages, IT professional courses, and visual storytelling.
