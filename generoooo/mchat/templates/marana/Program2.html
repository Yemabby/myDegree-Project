Program

Maranatha Institutes programs are designed for a new generation of storytellers and computer proteges: artists who share a passion for visual and performing arts across a wide array of media and want to learn by making their own projects in a hands-on, intensive program. Also, for those who have unquenched thirst for computer knowledge. Our students come from across the country and all over the world to learn and work together in Fine Arts, computer courses, professional courses, Degree programs, Conservatory One and Two-Year programs and short-term workshops*. In the creation of our curricula, we worked with faculty and professionals from around the world to develop the most effective curricula available to students. We designed intensive and accelerated alternatives to traditional visual and performing arts programs that provide a thorough grounding in a student’s chosen craft through project-based learning. Our programs balance the study of the craft with practical experience to train students to be the best in their chosen field. This is achieved by a rigorous schedule of classroom instruction, hands-on workshops, and immediate practical experience.

Our Philosophy

Learning to be a computer professionals, filmmaker or a visual storyteller is not done "by the numbers." It is done by immersion in the process and by knowing the "whys" that teach the "hows." It is done by hands-on experience, trial and error, feedback and correction, and questions and answers. Filmmaking and visual storytelling of all types—be it video game design, 3D animation, photography, musical theatre, or broadcast journalism—demands the integration of many kinds of knowledge. It is the integration of knowledge that distinguishes Maranatha Institutes. For example, in our filmmaking courses, students learn not only how to operate a camera, light a scene, or edit film, but they also learn how all the aspects of filmmaking relate to, interact with, and depend upon each other. We believe top quality education should be accessible to anyone with the necessary drive and ambition. The student provides the drive, thirst, and talent; we provide great instruction, equipment, and structured curriculum. Given the opportunity, it is the students' drive and creative ability that carries them forward.

Programs Offered

Maranatha Institutes is more than a computer institute or film school, rather, it is a conservatory of computer, visual and performing arts offering numerous programs that are designed to educate students in a wide variety of disciplines. It has become a creative proving ground for students around the world interested in learning the computer programs, craft of photography, video game design, musical theatre and 3D animation. We attract inspiring, experienced, professional faculty from IT world, media and entertainment industry to guide our students through learning the foundation knowledge and skills of these disciplines. At Maranatha Institute, students can enroll in the following programs to receive intensive, hands-on education that will provide them with practical skills and know-how while also advancing their understanding of computer languages, IT professional courses, and visual storytelling.
